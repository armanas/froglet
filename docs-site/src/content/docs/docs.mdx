## Core concepts

<div class="protocol-chain">
  <div class="protocol-step"><strong>Keypair</strong><span>Local identity. No account authority grants it.</span></div>
  <div class="protocol-step"><strong>Descriptor</strong><span>A provider declares its identity and service surface.</span></div>
  <div class="protocol-step"><strong>Quote</strong><span>The provider prices a concrete workload.</span></div>
  <div class="protocol-step"><strong>Deal</strong><span>The requester signs commitment to the quote.</span></div>
  <div class="protocol-step"><strong>Execute</strong><span>The provider runs the workload under declared limits.</span></div>
  <div class="protocol-step"><strong>Receipt</strong><span>The provider signs result, settlement state, and hashes.</span></div>
</div>
